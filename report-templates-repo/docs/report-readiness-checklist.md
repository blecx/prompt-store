# Report Readiness Checklist

Use this before publishing or sharing the report.

- Title and metadata are complete
- Audience and objective are explicit
- Scope boundaries are visible
- Method and evidence basis are described
- Findings are distinct from analysis
- Recommendations are actionable
- Risks and limitations are stated
- References are present or placeholders are clearly marked
- Confidentiality and distribution status are correct
- Executive summary matches the body
