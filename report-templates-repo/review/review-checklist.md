# Review Checklist

## Structural review
- report objective is clear
- audience is clear
- scope is explicit
- method is explained
- findings are separated from analysis
- recommendations are explicit

## Evidence review
- major claims are supported or labeled as inference
- assumptions are visible
- gaps are not hidden
- limitations are visible

## Recommendation review
- recommendations follow from findings
- trade-offs are stated
- feasibility is considered
- dependencies are noted

## Communication review
- executive summary matches body
- jargon is controlled
- repetition is reduced
- section order supports reading
