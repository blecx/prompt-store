# Publishing Readiness Checklist

## Front matter
- title page complete
- copyright page complete
- legal notices validated
- acknowledgments reviewed
- preface finalized

## Body
- chapter goals consistent
- terminology consistent
- no major unresolved placeholders
- tables and figures referenced in text
- trade-offs present where required
- failure modes present where required

## Back matter
- glossary complete
- references consistent
- appendices relevant
- index strategy decided

## Production
- manuscript builds
- filenames stable
- metadata captured
- licensing decision finalized
