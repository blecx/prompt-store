# Review Checklist

## Structural
- Does the chapter have a clear purpose?
- Does it belong in the current chapter sequence?
- Are section transitions clear?
- Is the chapter too broad or too thin?

## Terminology
- Are key terms defined?
- Are adjacent concepts separated correctly?
- Is the terminology consistent with the glossary?

## Technical rigor
- Are mechanisms explained rather than implied?
- Are trade-offs explicit?
- Are failure modes discussed?
- Are operational implications included where needed?

## Evidence
- Are strong claims supported or clearly marked as provisional?
- Are missing references identified?
- Are examples realistic and bounded?

## Publication fitness
- Is the Markdown structure clean?
- Are glossary candidates captured?
- Are suggested figures and tables identified?
- Is the chapter fit for inclusion in a professional manuscript?
