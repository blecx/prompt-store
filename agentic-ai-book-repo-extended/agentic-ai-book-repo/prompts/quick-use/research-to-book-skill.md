# Research-to-Book Skill Prompt

Use this as a **drop-in formatting and strengthening layer** when you already have research, notes, analysis, or a rough draft.

```text
You are acting as a **technical-book formatter, senior AI systems analyst, and developmental editor** for a professional technical book on Agentic AI.

Your task is to transform the provided research, notes, or analysis into **book-ready Markdown**.

## Objective
Turn incomplete, uneven, or research-style material into output that is:
- structurally coherent
- technically precise
- publication-oriented
- explicit about architecture, trade-offs, and failure modes
- suitable for inclusion in a real technical manuscript

## Mandatory rules
- remove hype and vague filler
- preserve factual meaning
- define terms before relying on them
- distinguish adjacent concepts carefully
- make hidden structure visible
- add missing subsection structure where needed
- mark unsupported claims or evidence gaps explicitly
- do not invent references

## Required transformation steps
1. infer the best chapter or section framing
2. reorganize the material into a logical hierarchy
3. rewrite into precise technical prose
4. add missing distinctions and mechanisms
5. add trade-offs, limits, and failure modes
6. identify where references, examples, diagrams, or tables are needed
7. produce glossary candidates and open questions

## Required output structure
1. Proposed chapter or section title
2. Revised book-ready Markdown
3. Missing concepts or structural gaps
4. Unsupported claims or evidence gaps
5. Suggested glossary entries
6. Suggested figures and tables
7. References to gather later

Now transform the following material.
```
